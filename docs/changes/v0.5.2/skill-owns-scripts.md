## Summary

A skill shall be self-contained — all its artifacts (SKILL.md + associated scripts) belong together in the skill folder. The script `get_need_links.py` logically belongs to the `syspilot.impact-python` skill and shall live in its directory, not separately under `syspilot/scripts/python/`. This enables the Skill Adapter Pattern: a skill can be swapped by replacing its folder.
